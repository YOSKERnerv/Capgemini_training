Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy quick dish to perfectly ruin the mood if eaten at dinner, but its best for morning , now i will share the recipe and ingredients in it, thankyou..

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approx 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter and oil
  • Optional fillings, cheeze, deiced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them with a fork until the yolks and whites are fully combined.
  2. Season the beaten eggs with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter and oil. Allow it to melt and coat the bottom of the pan.
  4. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet, tilting it to spread the eggs evenly across the surface.
  5. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for about 1-2 minutes until they start to set around the edges but are still slightly runny in the center.
  6. If you want to add fillings, sprinkle them over one half of the omelette at this point.
  7. Using a spatula, carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings, creating a half-moon shape.
  8. Continue cooking for another 1-2 minutes until the omelette is fully set and cooked through.
  9. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately. Enjoy your delicious homemade omelette!
